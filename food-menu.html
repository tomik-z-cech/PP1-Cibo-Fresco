Apetizers :

  • Bruschetta: toasted bread with fresh tomatoes, garlic, and basil
  • Arancini: fried rice balls stuffed with mozzarella cheese and ground beef
  • Carpaccio di Manzo: thin slices of raw beef with arugula, parmesan cheese, and lemon dressing
  • Prosciutto e Melone: slices of cured ham with fresh melon

Pizza :

  • Margherita: tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil leaves
  • Quattro Formaggi: mozzarella, gorgonzola, parmesan, and fontina cheeses
  • Diavola: tomato sauce, spicy salami, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil leaves
  • Funghi: tomato sauce, mushrooms, mozzarella cheese, fresh thyme leaves

Pasta :

  • Spaghetti Carbonara: spaghetti in a creamy sauce with pancetta, parmesan cheese, and black pepper
  • Lasagna: layers of pasta, bolognese sauce, bechamel sauce, and parmesan cheese
  • Linguine alle Vongole: linguine pasta with clams, garlic, white wine, and parsley
  • Penne all'Arrabbiata: penne pasta in a spicy tomato sauce with garlic and chili flakes

Desserts :

  • Tiramisu: layers of ladyfingers soaked in espresso and mascarpone cream
  • Cannoli: crispy pastry tubes filled with sweet ricotta cheese and chocolate chips
  • Panna Cotta: a creamy dessert made with milk, cream, and sugar, served with fruit or caramel sauce
  • Zabaione: a light custard made with egg yolks, sugar, and Marsala wine, served with berries or biscotti

Gealto :

  • Italian-style ice cream made with milk, cream, and various flavors such as pistachio, chocolate, or strawberry
